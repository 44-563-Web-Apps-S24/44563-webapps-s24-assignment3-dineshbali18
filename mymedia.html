Remo is a 2016 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film. It was written and directed by Bakkiyaraj Kannan and stars Sivakarthikeyan and Keerthy Suresh. The film is about an aspiring actor who falls in love with a doctor and disguises himself as a nurse to break her engagement.
